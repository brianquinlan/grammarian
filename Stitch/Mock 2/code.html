For a Level 5 Cleric, you have access to 3rd-level spells, which are quite potent. Here are some excellent divine options suitable for your level. Mass Healing Word is particularly useful for stabilizing multiple allies, while Spirit Guardians is a staple for battlefield control.

Mass Healing Word

Level 3 Evocation
Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier.

Spirit Guardians

Level 3 Conjuration
Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (15-foot radius) Components: V, S, M Duration: Conc. up to 10 mins

You call forth spirits to protect you. They flit around you to a distance of 15 feet for the duration, damaging enemies.

Revivify

Level 3 Necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (300gp) Duration: Instantaneous

Touch a creature that has died within the last minute. That creature returns to life with 1 hit point.
